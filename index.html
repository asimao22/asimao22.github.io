Layer.32

Layer.32 is an anonymous Instagram community curated by a group of fellow UMass Dartmouth students and myself.
The page focuses on creating a positive and safe environment for users to submit thoughts, concerns, self care tips, and any other commentary they wish to submit. Submissions are received via the site's direct messages, and the identity of the sender is kept completely secret for their comfort and privacy. The "burst" of new submissions is updated weekly in a series of nine submissions.

Soundbits

Created for an Information Design Class, this series was an exploration into how audio formats and music has evolved throughout roughly the last 100 years.

The Lost Angel

The Lost Angel was a two part photo series created during my time at CCRI. The first portion reflected on the story of the a lonely angel wandering amongst the woods. The second portion, entitled "Memento Vitae" continued upon this. Howver, the second portion allowed the angel to find happiness and their true purpose in life.

Ticket Redesign

A Ticketmaster concert stub was redesigned in order to provide a more visually interesting experience for the holder. The main concept introduced was to base it prominently on the typographic elements.
